How Culinary ties to ECS

Depending on if you own your own buissness, restaurant, cater, or type of chef you are, the computer science can change.

When you own a restaurant/buissness you should have a website with a menu, cotact information, about, and maybe a staff list. When you cater, you need to have a menu, contact information, pricing, event types, etc. Even if you cook from home, it could be nice to share new recipes online through a blog or website that you own or can edit.
